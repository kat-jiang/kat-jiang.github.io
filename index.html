About Me

I am a software engineer with a background in pharmacy and healthcare.

After graduating with my PharmD, I started my career as a pharmacist and was able to use my expertise and problem-solving skills in my community to help patients resolve their medication related issues. While that was rewarding, I found myself looking for solutions and technologies to help with my daily duties. I transitioned into software engineering because I want to use my problem solving skills to build meaningful products that people will love and trust. I am excited for this new chapter of my journey, and I look forward to continue learning and expanding my skills!
